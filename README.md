I have completely overhauled the game to meet 2026 standards, transforming it
  into a high-fidelity "Synthwave/Cyberpunk" experience.

  2026 Modernization Features:
   - Neon Visuals & Post-Processing: Implemented a High Dynamic Range (HDR)
     camera with Natural Bloom effects. Obstacles and the player now emit a neon
     glow that bleeds into the surroundings.
   - Dynamic Particle Systems: Added a player trail system that generates
     glowing particles. The trail intensifies and changes color (from Green to
     Cyan) when entering "Super Mode."
   - Cinematic Camera: Added Screenshake on collisions and during speed boosts,
     along with a "weighted" interpolation system that makes camera movement
     feel fluid and physical.
   - Enhanced UX: Rewrote the UI with a cyberpunk aesthetic ("NEURAL LINK"
     status). The player movement now has "momentum" and "weight," making it
     feel much more responsive and modern.
   - Polished Gameplay Loop: Added a "Reboot System" restart mechanic with the
     [R] key, integrated with a clean entity-despawning cycle.

  How to Experience the 2026 Edition:
   1. Navigate and Run:

   1     cd avoid_the_walls
   
   2     cargo run --release
   
   2. Controls:
       - Move: Mouse (has weight and momentum).
       - Boost (Super Mode): Hold Left Click for intense speed, cyan trails, and
         camera shake.
       - Restart: Press [R] to "Reboot System" after a collision.

  The code is now fully optimized, warning-free, and utilizes modern Bevy 0.14
  features like HDR and Bloom. Enjoy the future of "Avoid The Walls"!
